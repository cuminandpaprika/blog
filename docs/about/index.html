About

Hey, I’m Jack, a Software Engineer.


I’m currently building fraud detection systems at Mozn in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Alumni of University of Melbourne.


I’ve been building things ever since I can remember, from Lego Blocks as a 5 year old to Cities on Sim City 4 and most recently I’ve been building things with code. Mostly backend, mostly Go.

I’m fascinated by software craftsmanship, design, cooking, engineering philosophy and cloudy things. Looking to find the perfect cup of coffee☕.
